Agentic AI Book Buddy

An AI-powered multi-agent novel recommendation system built using Google Agent Development Kit and Gemini.

The Problem

Many existing book recommendation systems rely on popularity or surface-level metadata. Book Buddy focuses on understanding why a reader loved their favourite books and recommends novels that match their themes, tone, and writing style.

How It Works

1. Taste Extraction

User provides three favourite novels which are analysed for themes and tone.

2. Intelligent Search

A specialised agent searches the web for novels matching the profile.

3. Final Recommendation

The orchestrator selects one novel and explains why it fits the reader.

Architecture

preference_extractor_agent

Extracts genres, themes, tone, and pacing from the user’s favourites.

book_search_agent

Uses Google Search to find novels aligned with extracted preferences.

novel_recommender_orchestrator

Coordinates agents, tools, and memory to produce the final output.
